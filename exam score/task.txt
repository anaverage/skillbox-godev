Write a program that asks the user for the results of the Unified State Exam for three exams and checks whether he has entered the institute or not. 
The sum of passing points to the institute is 275.

Example of how the program works:

Enter the result of the first exam:
91
Enter the result of the second exam:
36
Enter the result of the third exam:
56
Total passing points: 275
Number of points scored: 183
You didn't get in.
